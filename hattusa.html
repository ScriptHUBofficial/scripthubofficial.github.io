MM Gear MM Gears is a Puzzle game that we think everyone will enjoy regardless of the player's age. In the game, players try to connect the correct gears onto rotating gears in order to make all of them turn and proceed to the next level. It is not enough to make them all turn for passing the level! Unlike the other gear games, without having an auto-connect function, our players will try to achieve a certain RPM for every level, using a real-time physics engine which will be the determining factor for level pass percentage. This game does not contain any violence.
